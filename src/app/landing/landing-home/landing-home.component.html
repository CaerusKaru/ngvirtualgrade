Welcome to Virtual Grade!

What is Virtual Grade?

Virtual Grade is a one-stop, integrated course administration system. It's meant for use by three distinct groups of people: students, graders, and administrators.

For Students

Virtual Grade for students means the ability to stay up-to-date on progress in any course on any device. It allows for a simplistic approach to submitting and reviewing assignments, requesting regrades, and viewing the upcoming course schedule.

For Graders

Virtual Grade for graders means the ability to comprehensively review any submission with ease and flexibility. It also means the ability to grade in an unbiased, anonymous manner to protect the integrity of the grading process.

For Administrators

Virtual Grade for administrators means the ability to easily create, modify, and track assignments across students and graders. It means that the entire process of regulating a course, no matter the size, can be done efficiently and with the right tools in mind. It means having the flexibility to configure assignments of various shapes, forms, and requirements.

Questions?

Virtual Grade was built to be an open-source, constantly evolving system. If you have any questions about its design, features, or setup, please contact the support team. The source code is currently available on GitHub, where all technical issues should be submitted. All other requests should be submitted to help@virtualgrade.cs.tufts.edu.
